## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Eden* Architect very seriously. If you have found a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

Publicly disclosing a vulnerability can put the entire community at risk. If you've discovered a security concern, please do not report it in the public issue tracker or in public forums.

Instead, please email **shiyindaxiaojie@gmail.com**. We will monitor this email and reply to you within 48 hours to acknowledge your report.

We will keep you informed of our progress towards a fix and the full announcement. We may ask for additional information to help us investigate the issue.

Thank you for helping keep Eden* Architect safe!
